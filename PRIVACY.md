# Privacy Policy

**X Follower Checker** does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

- All processing happens locally in your browser.
- Your X session credentials (CSRF token) are only used to authenticate requests to X's own API on your behalf — they are never sent anywhere else.
- Scan results are temporarily cached in `chrome.storage.local` to pass data between the content script and the side panel, and are cleared immediately after display.
- No analytics, no telemetry, no external servers.
